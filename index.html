Our approach

Local context

We conduct qualitative field research to learn about social problems and the demand for policies to address them.

Deep partnerships

We work with NGOs, governments, and international organizations from the beginning of program planning to build in prospective evaluations that inform policymakers in the organization and also contribute to the global knowledge base.

Credible evidence

We use randomized experiments to quantify the impact of programs and policies — and how they can be more effective.

Ethical research

We turn the microscope on ourselves to ask whether our research balances the risks and benefits to participants and how to conduct research safely in conflict-affected contexts.
